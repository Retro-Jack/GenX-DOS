Sammy Lightfoot

Sierra On-Line, 1983 — Warren Schwader — circus-themed platformer

Guide Sammy Lightfoot, a circus worker, through three perilous non-scrolling stages filled with trampolines, trapezes, spinning wheels, and fire rings. A Donkey Kong-style climbing game with a big-top theme. Completing all three stages restarts them at higher difficulty.

Getting started

  1. The game boots from disk. Press 1 for one player or 2 for two (hot-seat).
  2. Sammy appears at the bottom of the first circus stage.
  3. Reach the exit at the top to complete each stage.

Controls (joystick)

Sammy Lightfoot is a joystick game. In apple2js, move your mouse over the canvas to control direction. If a USB gamepad is connected, it takes over automatically (analog stick + buttons).

InputAction
Mouse left / right (or gamepad stick)Move Sammy left / right (no vertical control)
F1 or F2 (or gamepad A / B)Jump (tap to jump, hold to grab a rope)

Obstacles

HazardHow to pass
TrampolinesJump onto them to bounce higher. Landing position affects bounce height and direction.
Ropes / trapezesJump towards a swinging rope and hold the button to grab it. Release at the right moment to reach the next platform.
Spinning wheelsTime your movement to avoid the spokes. Getting hit costs a life.
Fire ringsJump through at the right moment — mistiming is fatal.
Rolling ballsJump over them as they roll across platforms.
GapsFalling through a gap costs a life. Jump or use trampolines to cross.

Strategy

  • Watch the timing. Most obstacles follow predictable cycles. Watch a full cycle before attempting to pass.
  • Hold the button for ropes. Just tapping won't grab — you must hold it while near the rope.
  • Commit to jumps. Once airborne, you can't change direction.
  • Trampolines are precise. Where you land on a trampoline affects where you go next.
