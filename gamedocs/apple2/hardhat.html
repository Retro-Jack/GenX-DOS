Hard Hat Mack

Electronic Arts, 1983 — Michael Abbott & Matthew Alexander — one of EA's first games

You're Mack, a construction worker on a building site. Complete objectives on three screens while dodging vandals, OSHA inspectors, falling bolts, and a rogue portable toilet. Jump between girders, ride lifts, and use springboards. Completing all three cycles the game at higher difficulty.

Getting started

  1. The game boots from disk. Press a key for keyboard control, or a joystick button for joystick. USB gamepads are also detected automatically.
  2. Complete the objective on each screen within the time limit.

The three screens

3 — FactoryCollect six boxes and drop each one into a processor.

Strategy

  • Learn the lift patterns. Moving platforms follow fixed routes — memorise when to jump on and off.
  • Jump early. Mack's jump has a short horizontal range — start before the gap, not at the edge.
  • Time the hazards. Inspectors and vandals patrol in patterns. Wait for them to pass.
  • Use springboards. They launch you to otherwise unreachable platforms.
