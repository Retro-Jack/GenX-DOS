Choplifter

Broderbund, 1982 — Dan Gorlin — one of the first rescue-mission games

Fly a helicopter behind enemy lines to rescue 64 hostages held in four barracks (16 per building). Shoot the barracks open, land to collect survivors, and ferry them back to your base. Enemy tanks, jets, and an alien spacecraft stand in your way.

Getting started

  1. The game boots from disk. Your helicopter sits at the base on the left.
  2. Fly right towards the enemy barracks.
  3. Shoot a barracks to release the hostages inside, then land nearby to pick them up.
  4. Fly back to base and land to drop them off. Repeat for all four barracks.

Controls (joystick)

Choplifter is a joystick game. In apple2js, move your mouse over the canvas to control the joystick axes:

InputAction
Mouse positionFly up / down / left / right
F1 (Open Apple)Fire weapons
F2 (Closed Apple)Change facing direction

Helicopter facing

The helicopter has three orientations — it can fly in any direction regardless of which way it faces:

FacingWeapon effect
Left or rightMachine gun fires horizontally — good for jets and barracks
Forward (towards you)Bombs drop straight down — good for tanks on the ground

Press F2 to cycle through facings.

Enemies

EnemyThreat
TanksDrive along the ground and fire upward. Face forward and bomb them from above.
JetsFly across the screen firing air-to-air missiles. Hard to hit — dodge or shoot quickly.
Alien spacecraftAppears in later stages. Aggressive and difficult to destroy.

Hostage rules

  • You carry up to 16 hostages per trip. Four barracks × 16 = 64 total.
  • Land level. If the helicopter is tilted when you land, hostages are crushed trying to board.
  • Don't land on hostages. Setting down directly on them kills them.
  • Friendly fire kills. Your own shots can hit hostages running in the open.
  • Clear enemies first. Hostages can be killed by enemy fire while exposed.

Strategy

  • Land carefully and level. A tilted landing kills the people you're trying to save.
  • Destroy tanks before landing. They fire at hostages on the ground.
  • Use forward-facing for ground targets. Press F2 to face the screen, then F1 to bomb.
  • Fly back often. Don't risk a full load — deliver 16 safely rather than lose them all.
