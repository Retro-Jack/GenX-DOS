Hunt the Wumpus

Gregory Yob, 1973 — from Creative Computing / 101 BASIC Computer Games

Track and kill the Wumpus — a fearsome beast lurking in a network of 20 caves arranged as a dodecahedron. Each cave connects to exactly three others. Listen for clues, avoid hazards, and shoot your crooked arrows before the Wumpus gets you.

Getting started

  1. The game loads via turbo tape and places you in a random cave.
  2. You are told which three caves connect to yours.
  3. Nearby hazards are announced with warning messages.
  4. Each turn, choose to move or shoot.

Commands

InputAction
MMove to an adjacent cave (enter the cave number)
SShoot a crooked arrow through a sequence of caves

Warnings

When you are one cave away from a hazard:

MessageMeaning
I SMELL A WUMPUSThe Wumpus is in an adjacent cave
I FEEL A DRAFTA bottomless pit is next door
BATS NEARBYSuper bats are in an adjacent cave

Hazards

HazardEffect
WumpusEnter its cave and it may eat you — or it may sleep through your visit and move to another cave.
Bottomless pits (×2)Fall in and die. No escape.
Super bats (×2)Grab you and drop you in a random cave. Not fatal, but you lose your bearings.

Arrows

  • You carry 5 crooked arrows.
  • When you shoot, specify a path of 1–5 caves for the arrow to travel through.
  • If the arrow enters the Wumpus's cave — you win.
  • If the arrow circles back and hits you — you die.
  • If you specify an impossible path (non-adjacent cave), the arrow picks a random valid direction.
  • If you miss, the Wumpus may wake up and move to an adjacent cave.
  • Run out of arrows without killing the Wumpus and you lose.

The caves

The 20 caves are connected like the vertices of a dodecahedron — each cave links to exactly 3 others. The layout is the same every game; only the starting positions of you, the Wumpus, pits, and bats are randomised.

Strategy

  • Map as you go. Note which caves connect where. The dodecahedron layout is fixed.
  • Triangulate the Wumpus. Move around smelling it from multiple directions to narrow down its cave before shooting.
  • Don't walk into a smell. If you smell the Wumpus, shoot into an adjacent cave rather than entering it.
  • Avoid bats. They randomise your position and waste turns re-orientating.
  • Count your arrows. Five shots is not many — make each one count.

Apple I keyboard

All input is uppercase. Type commands and cave numbers, then press Return.
