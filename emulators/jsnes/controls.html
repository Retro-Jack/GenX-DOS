NES — Controls

FCEUmm (libretro NES core) running in your browser via EmulatorJS. Released 1983 in Japan as the Famicom; the global cartridge library is ~700 titles, of which a dozen or so genre-definers sit in the bundled menu. Click the canvas first so the emulator receives keyboard input.

Notes

The Zapper light gun (Duck Hunt etc.) isn't wired up here. The games still run but can't register hits.

The browser may briefly mute audio until you click the canvas — that's the standard Web Audio autoplay policy, not a bug.

Emulator: FCEUmm (GPL-2.0, libretro) under EmulatorJS (GPL-3.0). Both shipped locally from this site; no network calls at runtime.
