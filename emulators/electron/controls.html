Acorn Electron — Controls

ElkJS emulates the Acorn Electron (1983). Games load from UEF snapshot files and start immediately.

Keyboard mapping

Most keys map directly. A few special keys:

KeyElectron key
AltFUNC
HomeCOPY
F12BREAK

Sound

Sound activates on the first keypress (browser autoplay policy). No toggle is needed after that.

Tips

  • Games load from snapshots — they start instantly, no tape loading wait.
  • If a game freezes, press F12 (BREAK) to reset.
