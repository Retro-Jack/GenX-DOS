Atari 7800 — Controls

The 7800's ProLine joystick has two action buttons. Player 1 is on the keyboard; the console switches are on function keys.

Player 1 joystick

KeyAction
↑ ↓ ← →D-Pad
ZButton 1 (Left)
XButton 2 (Right)

Player 2 joystick

KeyAction
W A S DD-Pad
QButton 1
EButton 2

Console switches

KeyAction
F2Select switch
F3Pause switch
F4Reset (start the game)
F12Soft reset (cycle the cart)

Notes

Most 7800 games sit on a "PRESS RESET" screen at boot — tap F4 to start play.

The 7800 also supports a Light Gun and Paddle controllers; JS7800 doesn't map these.

Mapping follows JS7800 v0.0.9 (GPL-2.0, raz0red/JS7800). Verify in upstream README if a control feels off.
