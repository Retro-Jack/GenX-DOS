Atari 2600 — Controls

The 2600 came with two joysticks and four console switches. Player 1's joystick is on the keyboard; the console switches are on function keys.

Player 1 joystick

KeyAction
↑ ↓ ← →Stick direction
SpaceFire button

Player 2 joystick

KeyAction
W A S DStick direction
Q or L AltFire button

Console switches

KeyAction
F1Power (toggle)
F4Select switch
F5Reset switch (start most games)
F7Colour / Black-and-White toggle
F8P0 difficulty (A / B)
F9P1 difficulty (A / B)
PPause emulation

Notes

Many 2600 games (Space Invaders, Asteroids, Centipede) require pressing F5 Reset to actually start play after the title.

Paddle games (Breakout, Kaboom!, Warlords) use mouse-X by default in Javatari — if a paddle game won't move, click on the canvas and use horizontal mouse motion.

Mapping follows Javatari.js v5.0.4 (AGPL-3.0, ppeccin/javatari.js). Press F12 in the emulator for the full upstream key reference.
